About

Hi there, I'm Kevin Dieu

I am junior studying Computer Science and Business Administration at the University of Southern California. I was born and raised in East Side San Jose with its thriving Vietnamese community.

Over the years, I've built confidential military software, prototyped future consumer technologies, drove product development at a startup, and launched my own ideas.

I am passionate about products, companies, and startups that push technological advancements for social change. Feel free to email me if you have any questions.
